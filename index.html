About the Institution

Our college was established in the year 1998. It is promoted and supported by Virudhunagar Hindu Nadar's Devasthanam, various Hindu Nadar's Mahamai Tharappus in Virudhunagar and other places and educational institutions of Virudhunagar. Our college is ideally located on the home land of Karmaveerar "Bharat Ratna" K. Kamaraj and our institution is one of the Virudhunagar's most recognizable landmark. Presently, Our College spans to 47.92 acres of land. This Institution has been approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University. The college offers 9 UG and 2 PG programmes. ECE, EEE, CSE, IT, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Bio-Technology Departments are accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. The College was conferred Autonomous status for the period of 10 years from 2020-2030 by University Grants Commission, New Delhi. Our institution is accredited by NAAC with "A" grade. Our institution is selected in a promising under ATAL ranking of Institutions in Innovation Achievement (ARIIA) ranking under private institution by Ministry of Education, Government of India. Our college is awarded as the Best Engineering College in South India for Industry Interface by Centre for Research and Education Growth (CEGR), New Delhi. In Career 360 Magazine Survey, our college got AAA ranking.
